Compliance

Regulatory-Compliant AI Betting


ChatBet is designed to operate exclusively through licensed sportsbook operators, ensuring full legal & regulatory compliance.

While ChatBet introduces a new way to monetize—through messaging apps—it functions purely as a communication channel.

  • No direct fund handling: All transactions go through the operator’s wallet.
  • No player data storage: ChatBet relays information but does not retain user data.
  • Operator-controlled markets: ChatBet mirrors the sportsbook’s existing offerings.
  • Adheres to Global Regulations: Compliance with iGaming, GDPR, and financial laws.

ChatBet is not a new platform—it’s a smarter interface layered on top of the operator’s system, simplifying betting while maintaining full regulatory alignment.
